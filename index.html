Gamagora is a video games creation project which takes place in Lyon every year. The aim is to create a cross-university student team and to build our own video games in 3 months. Our team won the 2nd price 'Customer Care Quality', awarded by EDF. 96 teams were competing. This experience deepened my enthusiasm for creation and improved my communication skills in a team.
